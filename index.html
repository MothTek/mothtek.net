About us

MothTek is a project to take advantage of the mass of information available to us
and apply it to the weird ideas we have.
Most of our projects exist because we thought it was cool or mildly useful, and we
hope they're useful to you too!

Using a questionable combination of Kicad, a cracked Altium install, and a lot of leaked
datasheets, and hella JLCPCB coupons, we produce designs featuring high speed PCIe and USB,
weird Chinese processors, and more :)

Check out our store, it might even be online when you read this!



Some of our projects:

  • ThinkMoth ExpressCard NVMe adapter
  • Thunderbolt 3 MXM docking station project
  • RPi CM4 Carrier board
