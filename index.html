Hi! We are MothTek, a small scale hardware design team operating out of Naarm/Melbourne.
We are committed to open source hardware, software, and end-user repairability.

Some of our projects:

  • Project Dark Leaf - ADAHRS
  • bugboard-1 - Phytium D2000 mITX motherboard
  • Thunderbolt 3 MXM docking station project
  • RPi CM4 Carrier board

Design services:

We primarily offer PCB design and small scale assembly services, utilizing industry
standard tools such as Kicad, Altium Designer, as well as the Cadence suite.
We believe in open source hardware, and are comitted to helping you build the ideal
hardware that you need. Whether its a custom Arduino clone, a Raspberry Pi Compute Module
carrier, a HDMI matrix switcher, or anything else you need made, we have the passion
and experience to make it a reality.

Who are we, and why?:

Hi, I'm moth! I'm based out of Naarm/Melbourne, and I am one of the founders of MothTek.
I have a passion for weird and odd hardware, and a large part of that is creating the weird hardware
that I want to see in the world. I have an extra interest in the world of Chinese domestic processors,
and when I'm not hyperfocused on , am usually found working on my obscure hardware wiki .
I also really, really like moths :3
